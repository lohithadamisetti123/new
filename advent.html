Adventure Destinations

1. Rishikesh, India

Rishikesh is the adventure capital of India. From white-water rafting to bungee jumping, it’s the place for adrenaline junkies on a budget.

Why Visit: River rafting in the Ganges, bungee jump from Mohan Chatti, hostels from ₹500/night.

Rating: ★★★★★ (5/5)

2. Pokhara, Nepal

Set beside a lake with a backdrop of the Himalayas, Pokhara is ideal for trekking, paragliding, and adventure tours at an affordable price.

Why Visit: Paragliding over Phewa Lake, Annapurna Base Camp treks, stays from ₹1000–₹1500/night.

Rating: ★★★★☆ (4/5)

3. Queenstown, New Zealand

Queenstown is globally known for adventure sports like skydiving, bungee jumping, and canyon swings. A thrill-seeker's paradise!

Why Visit: Try skydiving, Nevis bungee, mountain biking. Budget hostels start at ₹2500/night.

Rating: ★★★★★ (5/5)

4. Leh-Ladakh, India

A dream for bikers and trekkers, Leh-Ladakh offers high-altitude adventures, scenic mountain passes, and Buddhist culture.

Why Visit: Bike the Khardung La Pass, trek Markha Valley, budget stays from ₹800–₹1500/night.

Rating: ★★★★☆ (4/5)

5. Interlaken, Switzerland

Nestled between two lakes and surrounded by peaks, Interlaken is Europe’s hub for adventure lovers — from skydiving to canyoning.

Why Visit: Try canyoning, skydiving with Alpine views, hostels start from ₹5000/night.

Rating: ★★★★☆ (4/5)
